    What is a Hospital Management System?

    A Hospital Management System (HMS) is a comprehensive software tool used by hospitals to manage medical, administrative, and financial aspects of healthcare facilities.

    Key Features

    • Patient Records Management
    • Appointment Scheduling
    • Billing and Payment Processing
    • Inventory and Drug Management

    Benefits

    It ensures accurate record keeping, improves patient care, and increases the efficiency of hospital operations.
