    What is Pharmacy Management?

    Pharmacy Management Systems (PMS) are software applications used by pharmacies to streamline the management of pharmacy-related tasks, such as drug inventory, prescriptions, billing, and customer management.

    Key Features

    • Inventory Management
    • Prescription Handling
    • Billing and Invoicing
    • Customer Management

    Benefits

    The use of a Pharmacy Management System increases efficiency, reduces errors, and ensures compliance with regulations.
