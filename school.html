    What is a School Management System?

    A School Management System (SMS) is a software that automates administrative tasks, student records management, class schedules, and communication between students, teachers, and parents.

    Key Features

    • Student Enrollment
    • Class Scheduling
    • Attendance Tracking
    • Grades and Reports

    Benefits

    SMS enhances communication, reduces administrative workload, and streamlines school operations.
